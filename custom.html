St. Devin Catholic School Student Life

Here at St. Devin Catholic School, we value our students and how they spend their time here

What We Offer to Students:

At St. Devin, we offer a wide range of opportunities and resources to support students in their academic, personal, and social development. Students can expect a comprehensive curriculum that includes subjects such as English, mathematics, science, and social studies, along with a variety of elective courses that suites their interests and career goals. We often offer advanced placement or honors classes for academically motivated students seeking a more challenging curriculum. St. Devin also offers guidance and counseling services to support students college and career planning. With access to libraries, computer labs, and technology resources, students can engage in research, projects, and multimedia assignments.

What We Expect From Students:

  • Attendance: Come to School.
  • Bullying and Harassment: a zero-tolerance policy for bullying, cyberbullying, harassment.
  • Academic Honesty: If caught plagiarism or cheating, you will be suspend or expelled.
  • Dress Code: Wear the proper uniform.
  • Health and Medication Policy: Addressing safety and managing student health concerns, we provide free help at our medical center in our school handled by a professional doctor.

Courses Offered:

  • Biology: In this course, students delve into the fascinating world of living organisms, studying their structures and functions
  • Chemistry: The course offers an exciting study into atoms, molecules, and reactions. Students also learn about the principles of matter.
  • Physics: Through the course, students develop a understanding for the laws that the universe follows. From motion and energy to electricity and reflection, students explore all of these concepts through demonstrations and experiments.
  • Biotechnology: In this advanced elective course, students will explore the field of biotechnology. Through hands-on work, they gain an understanding in engineering techniques, DNA analysis, and microbiology.
